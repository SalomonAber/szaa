My music

In my early years, my family and I often travelled between Gernsbach Germany and London Great Britain. The piano however stayed in Germany. To get to Germany we used to set off in the dark shadow of the 3 a.m. sky. We would watch the sun climb as we navigated the corroded English country lanes, frequently tolled French motorways and never-ending German Autobahns. When, at 10 in the morning, we finally arrived we would stumble into our home in a sleepy haze. Whilst my parents choatically unpacked the car I would make a beeline for the piano.

NPR Tiny Desk Best

In an office as crowded as a beehive, a couple of carefully placed mics record bona fide performances from both distinguished and emerging artists. The limited room behind Bob Hope's desks allows the focus to be placed on musical expression. Despite the tight squeeze, Josh Rogosin manages to paint NPR's signature bright and crisp sound in what has to be the best recorded series of live concerts to date!
